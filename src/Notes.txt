.react-calendar targets the main calendar container element.
.react-calendar__tile--now targets the current date tile.
.react-calendar__tile--active targets the selected date tile.
.react-calendar__tile--hover targets the tile that's currently being hovered over.
.react-calendar__tile--active:enabled:hover targets the selected date tile that's being hovered over.
You can modify these styles to fit your own design preferences. For example, you can change the font family, font size, colors, borders, and more.
